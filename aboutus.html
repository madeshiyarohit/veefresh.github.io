Founder / Veefresh

I founded VeeFresh based on my personal struggle with recurring BV and the desire to help other women who have experienced the pain, anxiety, and depression that can come from chronic vaginal issues. After years of trial and error, I have finally found nature-based solutions I am willing to share with the world!

Our mission is to help women everywhere take back their confidence through community, education, and effective solutions.
